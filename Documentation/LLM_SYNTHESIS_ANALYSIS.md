## The Uncomfortable Question

Both models dance around this without stating it directly: **Is HIRM a theory or a framework?**

A *theory* makes specific predictions from specific mechanisms. A *framework* organizes observations and suggests where to look.

Currently, HIRM behaves like a framework wearing a theory's clothes. It has the *form* of specific predictions (8.3 +/- 0.6 bits, hysteresis signatures, critical exponents) but the *substance* depends on definition choices that aren't locked down.

This isn't fatal—many successful scientific programs started as frameworks and crystallized into theories. But it means the current priority should be **locking definitions** rather than adding more mathematical machinery.
