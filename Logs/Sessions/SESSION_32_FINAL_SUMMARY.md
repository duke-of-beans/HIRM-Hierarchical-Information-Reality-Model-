- Large files (600+ lines) trigger crashes
- Need to consistently chunk writes <200 lines
- Performance warnings underestimated actual risk
- Should have chunked from start, not just after crash

**2. Tool Call Budget**
- Exceeded 3-call guideline significantly (~45 calls)
- Crash recovery required many small operations
- Balance between safety (more calls) and efficiency (fewer calls)
- BUILD_STATUS updates should be more frequent but briefer

**3. Time Estimation**
- "Single session" optimistic for 12,000-word integration
- Crash recovery added ~1 hour
- Quality verification needs dedicated time
- Should plan 4-5 hours for comprehensive document assembly

### Process Improvements for Next Time
