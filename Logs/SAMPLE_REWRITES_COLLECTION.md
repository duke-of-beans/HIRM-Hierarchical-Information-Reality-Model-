**WRONG:** "We propose a framework that..."  
**RIGHT:** "For a century, [field A] pursued [question X] while [field B] pursued [question Y]..."

**WRONG:** "Multiple lines of evidence suggest..."

**WRONG:** "May provide insights into..."

## COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

### Pitfall 1: Vague Integration Claims
**BAD:** "HIRM integrates insights from multiple theoretical frameworks"  
**GOOD:** "HIRM unifies IIT (Phi component), HOT (R component), GNW (neural architecture), FEP (optimization), and quantum measurement (SRID mechanism)"

### Pitfall 2: Burying the Lead
**BAD:** Save unification for Discussion section  
**GOOD:** State unification in first paragraph of Abstract

### Pitfall 3: Defensive Positioning
**BAD:** "While some might consider quantum consciousness speculative..."  
**GOOD:** "SRID resolves measurement problem through information geometry, avoiding consciousness-causes-collapse while explaining observer role"

### Pitfall 4: Missing Convergence Emphasis
**BAD:** "C_critical = 8.3 bits from holographic bound"  
**GOOD:** "C_critical = 8.3 bits emerges independently from: (1) holographic bounds, (2) RG fixed points, (3) working memory, (4) empirical PCI—extraordinary cross-validation"

### Pitfall 5: Technical Jargon Without Context
**BAD:** "The renormalization group beta function exhibits..."  
**GOOD:** "Why brain criticality correlates with consciousness: The renormalization group beta function exhibits..."
