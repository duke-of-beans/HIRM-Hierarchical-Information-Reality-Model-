- Sharp threshold (not gradual decline) in subjective reports
- Hysteresis: Different thresholds for induction vs. emergence

**EMPIRICAL SUPPORT (NEW):**

- LOR occurs at ~1 ug/ml higher Ce than ROR
- "Neural inertia cannot be solely explained by pharmacokinetics"
- Bistability mechanism confirmed across multiple studies

**Prediction 6.4:** Microtubule-stabilizing drugs (e.g., epothilone B) should:

**Evidence:** Wiest et al. (2024) showed epothilone B delays unconsciousness - partially confirming this prediction.

**Prediction 6.5:** Nuclear spin isotope effects (Li et al., 2018):
