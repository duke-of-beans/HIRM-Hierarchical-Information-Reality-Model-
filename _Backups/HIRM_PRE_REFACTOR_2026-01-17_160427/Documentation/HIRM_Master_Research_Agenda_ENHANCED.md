dθ/dt = -κ·θ + σ·ξ(t)  (restoring force)
Steady state: <θ²> ≈ σ²/2κ → small drift

Where:
- θ: Angle between current intention and target

- κ: Anchor strength (viewing frequency × emotional salience)
- ξ(t): White noise (random fluctuations)
